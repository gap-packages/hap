  [1X7.9 [33X[0;0YCohomology rings of finite fundamental groups of 3-manifolds[133X[101X
  
  [33X[0;0YA  [13Xspherical 3-manifold[113X is a 3-manifold arising as the quotient [22XS^3/Î“[122X of the
  3-sphere [22XS^3[122X by a finite subgroup [22XÎ“[122X of [22XSO(4)[122X acting freely as rotations. The
  geometrization  conjecture,  proved  by Grigori Perelman, implies that every
  closed  connected 3-manifold with a finite fundamental group is homeomorphic
  to a spherical 3-manifold.[133X
  
  [33X[0;0YA  spherical  3-manifold [22XS^3/Î“[122X has finite fundamental group isomorphic to [22XÎ“[122X.
  This fundamental group is one of:[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[22XÎ“=C_m=âŸ¨ x | x^mâŸ©[122X ([12Xcyclic fundamental group[112X)[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[22XÎ“=C_mÃ—  âŸ¨  x,y | xyx^-1=y^-1, x^2^k=y^n âŸ©[122X for integers [22Xk, mge 1, nge 2[122X
        and [22Xm[122X coprime to [22X2n[122X ([12Xprism manifold case[112X)[133X
  
  [30X    [33X[0;6Y[22XÎ“=  C_mÃ—  âŸ¨ x,y, z | (xy)^2=x^2=y^2, zxz^-1=y, zyz^-1=xy, z^3^k=1âŸ©[122X for
        integers [22Xk,mge 1[122X and [22Xm[122X coprime to 6 ([12Xtetrahedral case[112X)[133X
