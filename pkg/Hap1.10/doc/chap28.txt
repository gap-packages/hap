  
  [1X28 Some functions for accessing basic data[0X
  
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XBoundaryMap(C)[0m
  
  Inputs  a  resolution,  chain  complex  or cochain complex C and returns the
  function C!.boundary.
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XBoundaryMatrix(C,n)[0m
  
  Inputs  a  chain  or  cochain complex C and integer n>0. It returns the n-th
  boundary map of C as a matrix.
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XDimension(C) [0m
  [10XDimension(M) [0m
  
  Inputs  a  resolution,  chain  complex  or cochain complex C and returns the
  function C!.dimension .
  
  Alternatively,  inputs an FpG-module M and returns its dimension as a vector
  space over the field of p elements.
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XEvaluateProperty(X,"name") [0m
  
  Inputs  a  component  object  X (such as a ZG-resolution or chain map) and a
  string  "name"  (such as "characteristic" or "type"). It searches X.property
  for the pair ["name",value] and returns value. If X.property does not exist,
  or if ["name",value] does not exist, it returns fail.
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XGroupOfResolution(R)[0m
  
  Inputs a ZG-resolution R and returns the group G.
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XLength(R)[0m
  
  Inputs  a resolution R and returns its length (i.e. the number of terms of R
  that HAP has computed).
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XMap(f)[0m
  
  Inputs  a  chain  map, or cochain map or equivariant chain map f and returns
  the mapping function (as opposed to the target or the source of f) .
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XSource(f)[0m
  
  Inputs  a chain map, or cochain map, or equivariant chain map, or FpG-module
  homomorphism f and returns it source.
  ::::::::::::::::::::::::
  [10XTarget(f)[0m
  
  Inputs  a chain map, or cochain map, or equivariant chain map, or FpG-module
  homomorphism f and returns its target.
