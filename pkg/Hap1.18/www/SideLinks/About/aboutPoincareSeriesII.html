 About HAP: Poincare series for groups of order 64

The following commands compute Poincare functions for most of the groups of order 64. A few groups are omitted from this general calculation as they need too much RAM. Again, we have no general proof that our algorithm produces a series which is correct in arbitrarily high degree. Proofs must be given case by case.
gap> L:=[60,67,73,80,88,99,199,206,213,215,216,224,227,232,241,242,266];;
gap> for G in AllSmallGroups(64) do
> if not IdSmallGroup(G)[2] in L then
> Print("\n Small group ",IdSmallGroup(G), " has Poincare series \n",
> PoincareSeries(G), "\n");
> fi;
> od;
